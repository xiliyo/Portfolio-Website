PAYMENTS

✖︎ Full payment upfront before I start the commission (with the exception of Vtuber Models and MV Services as those tend to run more expensive & are very time consuming).

✖︎ Commercial Use is 150% of the original pricing. When requesting, please state what you’ll be using the work for explicitly, preferably with links if applicable.


REVISIONS

✖︎ Depending on price point, amount of revisions may vary! Anything below 20$CAD does not include revisions.

20$CAD+ includes one revision.60$CAD+ includes two revisions.80$CAD+ includes three revisions.

✖︎ Any major changes that require more than an hour of fixing may go for an additional price.


DEADLINES AND DELIVERY

✖︎ Usual return time for illustrations is 2 weeks, depending on the order it can go to 3 months maximum. Vtuber models and MV commissions are an exception.

✖︎ The artist will provide a draft sketch for all illustrations within 2 weeks of payment. If the artist fails to meet this deadline, the client can request to be refunded without receiving the artwork.

✖︎ Rush commission fees can vary due to circumstance, but will usually be 200% of the original price point. Please contact for more inquiries.


USE

✖︎ The client always required to credit the artist [@xiliyo/@xiliyomaeryposa] when using the artwork for either commercial or personal usage. The client posting drafts of the work is allowed so long as the artist is credited!

✖︎ Personal use is granted with all commissions. This counts as using the artwork for a profile picture, slight edits, banners, etc. Anything that you don’t directly profit from.

✖︎ Commercial use is any distribution of the artwork for money. There is a 150% fee for commercial use of the artwork.

✖︎ Never use my art for AI training, NFTs, or anything Cryptocurrency related.

✖︎ The price for commercial use of the commission depends on the artwork, but usually is 150% of the standard pricing. Please DM or Email for more inquiries.

The client has violated or disregarded the artist's terms of service.There has been an absence of communication from the client to the artist for a duration exceeding three weeks.The artist does not supply a preliminary draft or secure sketch approval within two weeks from the initiation of the project after payment is received and confirmed.
